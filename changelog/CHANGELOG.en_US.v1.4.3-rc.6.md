# Changelog for v1.4.3-rc.6

## Film Processing

- Improved tonal balance — midtone detail is better preserved and highlight/shadow clipping is reduced.

## Languages & Fonts

- Added native font support for Traditional Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, and Tibetan — text in these languages no longer falls back to system fonts.
- Filled in missing translations across Traditional Chinese, French, German, Polish, Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Hindi, Tibetan, and Korean.
